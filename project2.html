Autonomous Rail Rapid Transit Application Prototype

Experience the innovative application prototype for Autonomous Rail Rapid Transit, which provides a glimpse into the future of efficient and technology-driven public transportation system.

Building A Unique Experience

Through the utilization of Human Computer Interaction elements as well as UI/UX knowledge, a seamless and powerful prototype was given birth.

The Autonomous Rapid Rail Transit application prototype is developed according to the ART plan by Sarawak Metro, which is part of Kuching Urban Transportation System (KUTS) project.

Tech Stack

  • Figma

What Is Inside The Application?

The Autonomous Rapid Rail Transit Application Prototype provides features such as Maps, Ticket Purchase, Real-Time Tracking and so on. In addition, Accessibility features are included to provide inclusion.
